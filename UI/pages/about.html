TeamWork




TeamWork is a platform that will facilitate organisations' employees to strengthen their collaboration and achieve their goals.

How it works



An employee creates an account on TeamWork and from then on they can post articles to be viewed by their colleagues, they can edit those articles as many times as they want, they can also share their opinions about other employees' articles.

I am developing this platform as a project that is part of the application for the Andela fellowship program in Kigali, Rwanda where i hope to learn and collaborate with many different people and change my community and the world one line at a time.
